Al-Shifa provides services in general medicine, women’s health, and dentistry.
Although there may be some specialty referrals, the clinic’s major emphasis is on primary health care. Not only do we provide quality healthcare in primary care,
but we also have a network of specialists who provide quality health care in other areas.
These specialists provide specialty care through Al-Shifa Clinic as well as through their own offices.
